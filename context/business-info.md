## Organisation Overview

The Evolved All Female Gym is a women-only strength training facility based in West End, Brisbane.

The gym delivers structured, progressive, science- and evidence-based strength training tailored to women across life stages:

- Teen  
- 20–30s  
- Planning Pregnancy  
- Pregnancy  
- Postpartum  
- Perimenopause  
- Post Menopause  

The core philosophy is strength-first, performance-driven, and longevity-focused.

Programming decisions are rooted in biomechanics, physiology, and measurable progress — not trends.

The gym operates as a premium, high-touch coaching environment rather than a high-volume fitness facility.

---

## Products / Services / Focus Areas

Primary focus areas:

- Small Group Personal Training (SGPT)
- 1:1 Personal Training (PT)
- Hybrid premium membership (Fast Track)
- Structured onboarding pathways for new members
- Long-term strength and performance development

The service model is built around recurring coaching relationships rather than transactional programs.

The Evolved is positioned as:

Coaching quality, evidence-based practice, and retention are foundational.
